Welcome!



EcoVend Promises to reduce plastic waste, empower responsible choices, and foster a cleaner, healthier planet. Our brand is entirely dedicated to delivering convenience without compromising on environmental impact, ensuring every interaction with us is a step towards a more sustainable future.



Our mission is to revolutionize the vending machine industry by offering innovative and accessible alternatives to single-use plastic items. We are committed to providing plastic-free vending machines that empower individuals to make sustainable choices, ultimately reducing plastic waste and fostering a cleaner and healthier world. At EcoVend, we believe that every small action can make a significant impact, and we are dedicated to leading the way towards a greener and more sustainable planet.


Introducing the EcoVend Vending Machine!

The first prototype model of the EcoVend vending machine dispenses environmental friendly alternatives to single-use plastic items. These vending machines offer reusable water bottles, bamboo toothbrushes, metal straws, cloth shopping bags, and other sustainable products. These innovative machines encourage individuals to make sustainable choices, reduce plastic waste, and contribute to a cleaner and healthier world. Adding to this, the vending machine offers the option to dispose of plastic goods in a seperate compartment in exchange for a more sustainable product.
